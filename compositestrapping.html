Composite Strapping

LIFEX® branded Composite Corded Straps have been the reference strapping solution since its first introduction to the global corded strapping market. Strong, durable, light, easy to use, weatherproof, safe to use Lifex straps are the prefered composite corded strapping product in the market. This product combines the multiple advantages of both high tenacity industrial polyester and its extruded polymer shell to provide an easy and sustainable solution for cargo securing and cargo safety. 

What is Composite Strapping?

Composite strapping is a type of packaging strap that combines the strength of steel with the flexibility of plastic. It is made of a blend of high-strength fibers and polymers, such as polypropylene (PP) and polyester (PET), providing a robust yet lightweight alternative to traditional steel strapping. Composite strapping can come in various widths and thicknesses, tailored to fit specific uses and requirements.

Advantages

Excellent Break Strenght

LIFEX is produced 100% with high tenacity industrial polyester cords and it is highly reliable in any kind of securing, baling and palletizing applications.

Safe to Use

LIFEX does not cause any risk of damage during tensioning and injury while removing the strapping because it is made 100% out of polyester.

Delicate Packaging

LIFEX ensures no damage on the surface or at the corners of delicate products. It prevents scratches, does not corrode and stain.

Elongation and Elastic Memory

Elongation and elastic memory features of polyester cords allow LIFEX to be tensioned perfectly on any type of surface.

Manual Applications

LIFEX is a light and practical product. It can be applied and removed by hand in complete safety without extra precautions.

Endurance to Severe Weather Conditions

LIFEX mintains its shape and tension completely under rigorous weather conditions. It is resistant to high temperature, ultraviolet rays and moisture.

Application With Low-Cost Hand Tools

Compared to other plastic straps, LIFEX can be applied with simple light and trouble-free hand tools, which are very sturdy and economical.

Product Customization

Thanks to the leading edge technologies we use, you can customize and personalize your LIFEX products according to your needs by printing your brand, slogan or messages on security in three different colors.
