Containerization

Containerization composite strapping, also known as cord strapping or cordage, is a type of packaging material used to secure and stabilize goods during transportation and storage. Made from high-tenacity polyester yarns, containerization composite strapping is designed to provide maximum strength and durability while minimizing damage to packaged goods.

One of the key advantages of containerization composite strapping is its ability to absorb shock and tension, making it ideal for securing heavy and irregularly shaped loads. Unlike traditional steel strapping, composite strapping is non-corrosive, lightweight, and easy to handle, reducing the risk of injury to workers during handling and disposal.

In addition to its superior strength and flexibility, containerization composite strapping is also environmentally friendly and cost-effective. Because it is made from recyclable materials, it helps reduce waste and minimize the carbon footprint of shipping operations. Furthermore, composite strapping requires less maintenance and re-tensioning than traditional strapping materials, resulting in lower overall packaging costs.

Containerization composite strapping is available in a variety of widths, thicknesses, and break strengths to suit different packaging needs. Whether you are shipping fragile glassware, heavy machinery, or oversized pallets, composite strapping can provide the secure and reliable packaging solution you need to ensure the safe delivery of your goods.

For more information about the benefits of containerization composite strapping and how it can improve the safety and efficiency of your packaging operations, contact us today. Our team of experts can help you choose the right strapping solution for your unique packaging requirements and provide guidance on proper handling and storage techniques to ensure the successful transportation of your goods.

Benefits

Increased Safety

Composite strapping is designed to securely hold loads in place during transportation, reducing the risk of shifting or falling.

Durability

Composite strapping is made from high-strength materials such as polyester fibers and polypropylene, which are resistant to tearing and breaking under pressure.

Cost-effective

Composite strapping is a more affordable alternative to steel strapping, providing the same level of performance and reliability at a lower cost.

Lightweight

Composite strapping is much lighter than steel strapping, making it easier to handle and transport while still providing strong and secure support.

Weather Resistance

Composite strapping is resistant to UV rays, moisture, and extreme temperatures, making it suitable for outdoor and long-distance transportation.

Easy To Use

Composite strapping can be easily applied and tensioned using manual or pneumatic tools, offering a quick and efficient solution for securing shipments.

Environmentally Friendly

Composite strapping is recyclable and can be disposed of responsibly, reducing the environmental impact of packaging materials.
